Comparison Operators

  • == : Equal to (equality), does implicit (automatic) conversion
  • === : Equal value and equal type (strict equality)
  • != : Not equal
  • !== : Not equal value and not equal type
  • > : Greater than
  • < : Less than
  • >= : Greater than or equal to
  • <= : Less than or equal to

Logical Operators

  • AND (&&) Operator
  • OR (||) Operator
  • NOT (!) Operator
